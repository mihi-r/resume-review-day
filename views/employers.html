Resume Review Day Employer Registration

Resume Review Day is an event hosted by the Engineering and Applied Science Tribunal in order to help prepare students for the Career Fair. This event gives students the opportunity to sign up for minute intervals to receive resume feedback from industry leaders and gives students the ability to network with employers before the Technical Career Fair.

It is also used as a chance for employers to build student relations, as well as get face-to-face time with additional students that might be seeking a co-op at no cost to them.
